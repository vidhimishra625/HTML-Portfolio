The Best Movies According to Me

My top-3 Movies of all the time

Taare Zameen Par

This is one on the best movies that I have ever come across. It beautifully depicts the relation of a teacher and student.

3-Idiots

This is one of the best movies that is made on the life of Engineers. It is the one with which we all can relate.

Inception

It is one of the best sci-fi movies that has an enthralling plot right from the beginning to the end. It has marvelously depicted the concept of time travel.
